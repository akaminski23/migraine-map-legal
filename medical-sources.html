MIGRAINE MAP

Important: Migraine Map is a wellness tool designed to help you track patterns in your migraine experience. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations. The educational information presented in the app is based on published peer-reviewed research cited below. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical advice regarding your condition.
